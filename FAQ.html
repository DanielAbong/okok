FAQ’S AND MORE INFORMATION

If you did not find the answers you are looking for below, contact us and a local representative will be in touch shortly to help.



Is the $2,500 reservation deposit refundable? Yes, the reservation is refundable less a 10% fee..


Once i pay my deposit what's next? Once the reservation deposit is paid, a CargocontainersTP representative will be in touch to confirm shipping rates, logistics and to finalize your exterior color and upgrade selections. From there, a contract will be sent to you to complete the order and pay for the remainder of the unit and shipping.


Do i need building permit for Cargocontainer The Cargocontainer is considered a movable structure and is under 100 sqft which generally is exempt from permit requirements.


Do i need a foundation? The Cargocontainer can be set on a slab, foundation blocks or crushed gravel.


How do i connect my Cargocontainer to power? The Cargocontainer comes with 3’ attached exterior cord for you to connect to an extension cord and plug into a dedicated 110v circuit on the exterior of your house. If you choose the Heating/Cooling upgrade option you will need to have an electrician connect the Cargocontainer to a panel.


How do I upload Cargocontainer? The Cargocontainer is delivered on a flatbed truck to your property. A forklift, zoom boom or crane can be used to unload your Cargocontainer. It will be sitting on 4x4 blocks.


Can i have CargocontainersTP coordinate the logistica from the factory to my backyard? Yes! The cost for CargocontainersTP to coordinate is $1,000 and cost + 15%.


What is the warranty? The Cargocontainer comes with a 1 year warranty.


Can i customize the Cargocontainer? The styling and upgrades are fixed to create a streamlined and predictable process. Once it’s in your backyard, feel free to decorate it to your liking.


What f;ooring is included? Luxury vinyl plank flooring - Light.


What is the interior paint? Benjamin Moore Chantilly Lace.


How long does it take to get a CargocontainersTP? From design through to permits and completion it typically takes 10-12 months. Permits vary by jurisdiction and can be as quick as 1 month or as long as 6+ months. The quicker you begin the pre-construction phase the sooner you can have your home! It typically only takes us 10-12 weeks to actually build your home.


What can a CargocontainersTP be used for? A CargocontainersTP is simply a living space. A Home, a rental suite, office, ADU, guest house, granny suite, in-law suite, cabin or cottage.


What is a CargocontainersTP built with? A CargocontainersTP is built with an engineered steel frame and Corten Steel (container) siding that are precisely finished with high end materials and highly efficient systems.
